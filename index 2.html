ASSOCIATION ACTIVITIES

1- Art and Decoration: This event targets students of the basic stage to introduce them to the importance of environmental education, through the use of household items that are disposed of in a wrong and harmful way to the environment, and how to reuse them in decorating the house and making masterpieces from simple things by drying fruits that have expired, A decoration was made to be hung inside the house, and from here the child has learned the importance of proper use of these resources and may transfer the experience to his colleagues and friends to do it.

2- Land and Nature: It is a cultural symposium aimed at university students to discuss several ideas and topics, including: The current situation of Palestinian nature and the impact of the occupation on it, and the serious threats that occur as a result of occupation practices, and discussing how to achieve a balance between man and nature to preserve biodiversity. And talking about the responsibilities of all of us, students, individuals, associations and institutions, towards the earth and nature

BIOGRAPHY

The Environmental Care Association for Sustainable Development was established in Bidya / Salfit Governorate, under the Law of Charitable Societies and Civil Organizations No. (1) of 2000. It aims to protect the environment and public health from the dangers resulting from all kinds of waste, and to enhance the citizen’s ability to coexist with the environment, preserve it and defend it National rights to natural resources in cooperation with local and regional institutions and to carry out research, studies and environmental projects
