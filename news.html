The process of environmental awareness aims to spread the culture of preserving the environment and the risks that people can face. Environmental awareness means the extent to which man respects the components and elements of the living and non-living environment, and his responsibility towards preserving them from deterioration, as human practices have been characterized in recent times by the danger to the Earthâ€™s ecosystem, and caused many of the environmental problems that we live in at the present time. Environmental awareness goes beyond the selfishness of individuals to take care of their current lives only, to take care of the lives of future generations. The land is not owned by the current generation, but rather it is its responsibility to preserve it, to provide a healthy world free of environmental problems for the emerging generations, and to take the necessary measures to meet the current and expected environmental challenges. The future is a result of current environmental practices, in addition to the fact that environmental awareness includes instilling the values of human respect for the environment, by making it daily routine practices by parents and parents, the school, and the societal culture as a whole, to be rooted in the minds of the emerging generations and be part of their culture and lifestyle, as it helps Environmental awareness in realizing the consequences of human activities on the planet that humans, animals and plants share. Environmental awareness is one of the important educational means for preserving natural resources and limiting harm to them as a result of human actions and practices. Environmental awareness is instilled in individuals that their individual actions have a positive or negative impact on the environment to be reckoned with. Or damage the green vegetation. Environmental education at an early age contributes to instilling corrective environmental values in future generations, who will inherit the earth in the future, and the extent to which their environmental awareness affects improving the quality of life on the planet, and that their respect for the environment, its resources, and its elements, means a better life for them and their children in the future, which motivates them To rationalize the consumption of water and electricity, avoid harming animals, take care of home farming, and ways to manage and deal with waste, through their belief that they are part of the solution to many current environmental problems, and that caring for their neighborhoods and areas is enough to reflect a positive impact on the larger ocean.
