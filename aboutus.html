About Us

The Environmental Care Association for Sustainable Development was established in Bidya / Salfit Governorate, under the Law of Charitable Societies and Civil Organizations No. (1) of 2000, It aims to protect the environment and public health from the dangers resulting from all kinds of waste, enhance the citizen's ability to coexist with the environment and preserve it, defend national rights to natural resources in cooperation with local and regional institutions, and carry out research, studies and environmental projects.

Our Aim
  • 1- Protection of the environment and public health from the dangers resulting from all kinds of waste.
  • 2- Enhancing the citizen's ability to coexist with the environment and preserve it through the pursuit of food sufficiency and poverty alleviation
  • 3- Defending the national rights to natural resources, especially water and forests, and ensuring the protection of the soil and the preservation of these resources for future generations in cooperation with local and regional institutions.
  • 4- Conducting environmental research, studies and projects, creating environmental awareness and training at the grass-roots level in general and youth in particular, in order to facilitate effective environmental management.
  • 5- Assist rural and urban communities to access their lands and monitor land, environment and natural resources in a fair, equitable and sustainable manner
